As a white male, I am the face of dominant culture in the US. I am cis-gendered, able-bodied, and natively speak English. I hold a U.S. passport and a Master's degree.

These elements of my identity constitute layers of privilege that have shaped the way I move through the world. I acknowledge my positionality in order to be vigilantly, critically aware of how I am situated in the work I do.
